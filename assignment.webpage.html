Biography

Ariana Grande is an American singer, songwriter, and actress. She was born on June 26, 1993, in Boca Raton, Florida, is an American singer, songwriter, and actress. She began her career at a young age, gaining recognition for her performances on Broadway and later transitioning to television with roles in Nickelodeon series like "Victorious" and "Sam & Cat." Grande's musical journey took off with the release of her debut album "Yours Truly" in 2013, followed by successful albums like "My Everything," "Dangerous Woman," "Sweetener," and "Thank U, Next."
